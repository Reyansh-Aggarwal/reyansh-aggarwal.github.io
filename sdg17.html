PARTNERSHIPS FOR THE GOALS

The Aim

SDG 17 emphasizes the importance of strengthening the global partnership for sustainable development. It highlights the need to mobilize financial resources, enhance technology transfer, and build capacity to support sustainable development efforts worldwide. Key strategies include improving international trade systems, promoting fair and equitable partnerships, ensuring policy coherence, and increasing the availability of high-quality data. The goal is to foster collaborative efforts among governments, private sectors, and civil society to achieve the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and address global challenges effectively.

Progress

Global partnerships for sustainable development encompass key areas such as finance, technology, trade and data. There are mixed trends in mobilizing financial resources for development, expanding internet connectivity and and strengthening statistical systems. However, a substantial $4 trillion annual investment gap for developing countries to achieve the SDGs, persistent and crippling issues such as unprecedentedly high external debt levels, and limited access to online connectivity in low-income countries underscore the need for sustained collaboration and enhanced cooperation and support in a landscape of worsening international cooperation and geopolitical tensions.

Key Actions

  • Mobilizing Financial Resources of Developing Countries

  • Promoting Development and Transfer of Technology

  • Supporting National Plans in Developing Countries

  • Promoting Fair Trade

  • Enhancing Policy Coherence

  • Encouraging and Promote Partnerships

  • Improving Data Collection and Monitoring
