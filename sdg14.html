LIFE BELOW WATER

The Aim

SDG 14 aims to conserve and sustainably use the oceans, seas, and marine resources. Key targets include reducing marine pollution, protecting marine and coastal ecosystems, minimizing ocean acidification, regulating fishing practices, conserving at least 10% of coastal and marine areas, and increasing scientific knowledge and research. These efforts aim to enhance the sustainability and health of marine environments and support the livelihoods that depend on them.
