LIFE ON LAND

The Aim

The SDG 15, "Life on Land," focuses on protecting, restoring, and promoting sustainable use of terrestrial ecosystems. Key targets include managing forests sustainably, combating desertification, halting and reversing land degradation, and halting biodiversity loss. It also emphasizes the importance of integrating ecosystem and biodiversity values into national and local planning, development processes, and poverty reduction strategies.
