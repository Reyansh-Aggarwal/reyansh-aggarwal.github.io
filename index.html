Transforming Lives Using Sustainable Development Goals

What are the
Sustainable Development Goals?

The Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), also known as the Global Goals, were created and adopted by the United Nations in 2015 as a universal call to action to end poverty, protect the planet, and ensure that by 2030 all people enjoy peace and prosperity.

Transforming lives through the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) involves addressing critical global challenges such as poverty, inequality, and climate change. The SDGs provide a comprehensive framework to improve living standards worldwide by fostering sustainable development and resilience. By focusing on areas like education, health, and gender equality, the SDGs empower individuals and communities, promoting a more inclusive and prosperous future.
