CLIMATE ACTION

The Aim

The UNDP's Climate Action page emphasizes Goal 13 of the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), which focuses on urgent measures to combat climate change and its impacts. This goal includes targets to enhance resilience to climate-related hazards, integrate climate change actions into national policies, and build capacity for effective climate planning and management globally. The page highlights initiatives promoting sustainable practices among businesses and supporting countries in adopting policies that foster sustainable tourism and economic growth while preserving local cultures and environments. These efforts aim to mitigate climate risks, enhance adaptive capacity, and promote sustainable development worldwide.

Progress

Climate records were shattered in 2023, with the world watching the climate crisis unfold in real time. Communities around the world are suffering the effects of extreme weather, which is destroying lives and livelihoods on a daily basis.
